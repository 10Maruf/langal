Use Case Description

Use Case No

UC-12

Use Case

Read Agriculture News

Goal

To provide farmers with the latest news and updates in the agriculture sector.

Preconditions

Farmer is logged into the system.

Success End Condition

Farmer has access to a feed of relevant agricultural news.

Failed End Condition

Farmer is unable to view the news feed.

Primary Actors

Farmer

Secondary Actors

System

Trigger

Farmer navigates to the "News" section.

Main Success Flow

Step

Action

1

Farmer opens the "Agriculture News" page.

2

The system displays a list of recent news articles.

3

Farmer can click on an article to read the full content.

Alternative Flows

Step

Branching Action

2a

No News Available: If there are no news articles, the system displays a message.

Quality Requirements

Step

Requirement

1

The news feed should be updated regularly.

2

The articles should be from reliable sources.
